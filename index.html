Project

Noa

Noa helps you lower your blood pressure by tracking the habits that shape it.

No need to type or sync anything. Just pointing the phone at the blood pressure monitor, and it reads the numbers for you. From there, it's about the slow work: better sleep, daily movement and eating healthier.

Project

Strive

Strive is a social fitness app that helps you build a consistent gym habit through accountability with friends. Check in at your registered gyms using location-based verification, track your progress with streaks and visual heatmaps, and stay motivated with real-time notifications when friends check in.
